Privacy Policy for BoldDare – Truth or Dare

Effective Date: 25 May 2025
Last Updated: 25 May 2025

1. Introduction

BoldDare Studios (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game BoldDare – Truth or Dare (the “App”), available on the Google Play Store. It also tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

By installing or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App and discontinue its use.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data – any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (e.g., email address, advertising ID).
  • Non-Personal Data – information that does not identify you directly (e.g., device language, coarse location).
  • Processing – any operation performed on Personal Data (e.g., collection, storage, analysis).
  • Controller – BoldDare Studios, the entity that decides why and how Personal Data is processed.

  1. Provide and maintain the App (core gameplay, in‑app purchases).
  2. Improve and personalize gameplay (suggesting dares you might like).
  3. Analytics and performance monitoring.
  4. Advertising and cross‑promotion within the App.
  5. Content moderation – anonymized prompts are sent to OpenAI to ensure safety.
  6. Legal obligations – comply with laws, prevent fraud, enforce Terms of Service.

6. Sharing Your Information

We share data only as necessary with:

  • Google Play Services (Play Billing, GAID)
  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config)
  • Google AdMob (ads; see their policy)
  • OpenAI, LLC (AI content generation & moderation)
  • Service providers under confidentiality agreements (cloud hosting, customer support)

We do not sell Personal Data.

7. Advertising & Monetization

The App displays banner and interstitial advertisements through Google AdMob. Personalized ads use your Advertising ID. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings › Privacy › Ads).

In‑app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing; we do not receive your payment card details.

8. Data Retention

  • Gameplay analytics: 24 months (aggregated thereafter).
  • Crash logs: 90 days.
  • Purchase history: retained as long as required for accounting (up to 10 years under EU law).
  • User‑generated content: until you delete it or request erasure.

9. Security

We implement industry‑standard safeguards (TLS encryption in transit, secure Google Cloud storage, least‑privilege access). However, no system is 100% secure.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access the Personal Data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate data;
  • Delete your data;
  • Restrict or object to processing;
  • Data portability;
  • Withdraw consent (for ads) at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@bolddare.app.

For EU/EEA residents, you may lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority. For California residents, you can request information about categories of Personal Data disclosed for business purposes (CCPA).

11. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and should not be used by them. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children. If a parent believes we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us for deletion.

12. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed on servers outside your country (e.g., United States). We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and equivalent safeguards to protect EU data.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you by updating the “Last Updated” date and, for material changes, via an in‑app notice or Google Play console update. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
