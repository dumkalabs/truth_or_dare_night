1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing to play Truth or Dare Night. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in our mobile application published on Google Play.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data such as your name, email, or age.

We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, files, or location.

However, we may collect limited technical data automatically to operate and improve the app:

  • Device type and OS version: To understand our user base and ensure compatibility.
  • Gameplay interactions: Anonymous votes on which content is liked or disliked to help us improve our questions and dares.
  • Crash logs and usage analytics: To identify bugs and enhance app performance and user experience.

3. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use third-party services to help us analyze app performance:

  • Firebase Analytics for aggregated usage statistics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash diagnostics.

This data does not identify you personally. For more information on how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy & Terms. You can control your experience by opting out of analytics tracking through your mobile device's settings (usually under "Settings > Google > Ads" or "Settings > Privacy > Ads").

4. Advertising

Truth or Dare Night shows ads using Google AdMob. Ads are non-personalized, meaning your personal data is not used for ad targeting. For more information, you can review Google's policies on advertising.

5. In-App Purchases

We offer optional in-app purchases processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not receive, process, or store your payment information.

6. AI Content Generation

Some dares may be dynamically generated using OpenAI technology. No user data is stored or used to train the AI models. For more details on their data handling, please see the OpenAI Privacy Policy.

7. Age Restriction

This app is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with information, please contact us at dumkalabs@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing or profiling purposes. Data is only shared with the following integrated service providers for the sole purpose of operating our app:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google)
  • AdMob (Google)

9. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to safeguard the limited, non-personal data we collect from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

10. Data Retention

We retain the technical data collected (such as crash logs and anonymous usage analytics) only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, such as maintaining and improving the app. This data is periodically deleted.

11. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your privacy:

  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics and non-personalized ad tracking by adjusting the privacy and ad settings on your mobile device.
  • Right to Data Deletion: Although the data we collect is anonymous, you can request that we reset any analytics data associated with your device's installation. Please email us at dumkalabs@gmail.com with your request.
  • Right to Ask Questions: For any privacy-related concerns or questions about our practices, please contact us.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
